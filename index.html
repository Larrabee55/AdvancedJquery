I've lived in Utah my whole life. I've grown to love this place with the beautiful mountains and gorgeous lakes. I learned there is so much to do here and have picked up so many hobbies.

Soon after I joined a few of my coworkers in a small league. I did so good I had someone else pick me up to play their tema one one of the hardest leagues in Utah. I still play on that league to this day.

Golfing is one of my high school sports that I still play every year when the weather is right. It's such a fun and relaxing game to play with friends.

Riding my Motorcycle is my favorite hobby out of all of them. I get to do it everyday that the weather is good. It is a very thrilling way to get from place to place. When I'm not just using it to get from place to place I go on rides with my friends. My favorite ride is going up the mountains. There are so many fun turns and curves, not to mention how beatiful it is up there.
